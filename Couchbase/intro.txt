# Couchbase Intro

1. Couchbase is a distributed document database (JSON), with all the desired capabilities of a traditional DBMS;
2. distributed SQL, transactions, ACID guarantees; and much more. It is a hosted service and a product for hybrid (on-premises and cloud) deployments of mission-critical applications.
